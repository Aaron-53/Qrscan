# **App Name**: QR Scanner Notifier

## Core Features:

- Camera Access: Access the device camera for QR code scanning.
- QR Code Scanning: Continuously scan for QR codes in the camera view.
- GET Request: Upon scanning a QR code, send a GET request to base URL/team/content of QR.
- Content Registered Popup: Display a small popup confirming 'content of QR registered' after successful GET request.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep Indigo (#3F51B5) to evoke a sense of trust and efficiency in data processing.
- Background color: Light Gray (#F5F5F5), to keep the interface clean and focus on the camera view.
- Accent color: Teal (#009688) for confirmation messages and visual cues, contrasting against the indigo to indicate successful registration.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a sans-serif font with a clean and modern appearance for clear readability in popup messages.
- Simple, geometric icons for loading and confirmation states.
- Camera view occupies most of the screen, with a small, unobtrusive popup for displaying messages.
- Fade-in animation for the confirmation popup.
